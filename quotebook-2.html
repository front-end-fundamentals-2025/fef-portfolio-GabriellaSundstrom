My second quotebook

In 2022, I was working full time at a chemistry lab. Although I grew to love the job and my collegues there, I knew I wanted to do somehting more creative in the future. After almost two years of working with something outside of my dream, I saved up a lot of money, but I started feeling empty, going to work every day with no bigger purpose. Since I still had almost a year left mixing solutions and pipetting, I thought to myself if I could somehow turn everyday bleakness into art.

The idea of making a quotebook had come to me before, but only vaguely. For the first time, I considered collecting quotes from all over my workplace, even the most mundane ones. Decorating them in inspiring ways, I hoped to document the fortgetibility of normal days, and bring them to life.
